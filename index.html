Hi, I’m Laura Ozoria

I was born and raised in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic and later moved to New York City to pursue my undergraduate degree at Bucknell University. I am currently double majoring in Computer Science Engineering and Film Studies. My primary goal throughout my studies is to explore the intersection of computer science and the arts. I’m drawn to this field for its versatility and its ability to span across diverse spaces, which I find inherently artistic. This dual focus will allow me to develop a well-rounded education, and I’m excited to build future projects around this vision. Currently, I’m working as a Software Developer on a color analysis research project, which has sparked my interest in computer vision and data visualization as potential career paths. I’m also diving into a game development project that challenges me to blend my love for art with my programming skills. I aim to make my work accessible and engaging for all audiences. Outside of my academic interests, I’m passionate about photography and game development, which you can explore further in different sections of this portfolio.
